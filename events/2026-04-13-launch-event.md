## Description

The ceremonial launch of the FP Launchpad centre (https://fplaunchpad.org/) at IIT Madras, accompanied by a full-day technical programme of talks. The theme is broad systems engineering, aligned with the [centre's goals](https://fplaunchpad.org/charter), rather than functional programming specifically.

Academics, researchers, and students working on functional programming, PL, and systems from across India are invited. Expected attendees from IITB, IITD, IITGN, CMI, Ashoka, Krea, MSR, IITM Spinoffs, and other institutions.
